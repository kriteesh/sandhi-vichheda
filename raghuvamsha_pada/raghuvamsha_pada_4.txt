

    anvayam:

    saH guruNA dattam rAjyam pratipadya dinAnte savitrA nihitam tejaH
    pratipadya hutAshanaH iva adhikam babhau


    dilIpa ana.ntaram rAjye pratiShTitam tam nishamya pUrvam rAj~nAm
     hR^idaye pradhUmitaH agniH iva utthitaH


    purUhata dhvajasya iva tasya nava abhyutthAna darshinyaH ut ayana
    sa.msaktayaH sa prajAH prajAH nana.nduH


    dvirada gAminA tena samam eva pitryam si.mhAsanam akhilam ari 
    maNDalam ca dvayam samAkrAntam


    padmA svayam adR^ishyA kila cChAyA maNDala lakhyeNa padma
    AtapatreNa sAmrAjya dIkshitam tam bheje


    sarasvatI ca kAle kAle vandiShu parikalpita sAnidhyA stutyam
    [tam= him - raghu;] arthyAbhiH 
    stutibhiH upatasthe


    manu prabhR^itibhiH mAnyaiH bhuktA yadyapi rAjabhiH tathA
    api ananya pUrvA iva tasmin AsIt vasundharA


    sa hi sarvasya lokasya yukta daNDatayA manaH Adade na ati shIta
    uShNaH nabhasvAn iva dakShiNaH


    manda utkaNThAH kR^itAH tena guNa adhikatayA gurau phalena
    sahakArasya puShpa udgama iva prajAH


    naya vidbhiH nave rAj~ni sat asat ca upadarshitam pUrvaH eva
    abhavat pakShaH tasmin na abhavat uttaraH


    pa~nchAnAm api bhUtAnAm utkarSha.m pupuShuH guNAH nave
    tasmin mahI pAle sarva.n navam iva abhavat


    yathA prahlAdanAt chandraH pratApAt tapanaH yathA tathA eva saH
    abhUt anvarthaH rAjA prakR^iti ra~njanAt


    kAma.m karNa anta vishrAnte vishAle tasya lochane
    chakShuShmattA tu shAstreNa sUkShma kArya artha darshinA


    labdha prashamana svastham atha ena.m samupasthitA pArthiva shrIH
    dvitIyA iva sharat pa~Nkaja lakShaNA


    nirvR^iShTa laghubhiH meghaiH mukta vartmA suduHsahaH
    pratApaH tasya bhAnoH cha yugapat vyAnashe dishaH


    vArShika.m sa.njahAra indraH dhanuH 
    jaitra.m raghuH dadhau
    praja artha sAdhane tau hi paryAya udyata kArmukau


    puNDarIka atapatraH tam vikasat kAsha cAmaraH R^ituH
     viDambayAmAsa na punaH prApa tat shriyam


    prasAda sumukhe tasmin candre ca vishada prabhe tadA
    cakShuShmatAm prItiH AsIt sama rasA dvayoH


    ha.msa shreNIShu tArAsu kumudvatsu ca vAriShu
    vibhUtayaH tadIyAnA.m paryastA yashasAm iva


    ikShu cChAya niShAdinyaH tasya goptuH guNa udayam AkumAra
    katha uddhAta.m shAli gopyaH jaguH yashaH


    prasasAda udayAt ambhaH kumbha yoneH mahA ojasaH raghoH
    paribhava Asha~Nki cukShubhe dviShatA.m manaH


    mada udagrAH kakudmantaH saritA.m kUlam udrujAH lIlA
    khelam anuprApuH mahA ukShAH tasya vikramam


 


    prasavaiH sapta parNAnA.n mada gandhibhiH AhatAH asUyaya
    iva tat nAgAH saptadhaiva prasusruvuH


    saritaH kurvatI gAdhAH pathaH ca AshyAna kardamAn
     yAtrAyai codayAmAsa ta.n shakteH prathama.n sharat


    tasmai samyak hutaH vahniH vAji nIrAjanA vidhau pradakShiNa
    arciH vyAjena hastena iva jaya.m dadau


    avAkiran vayo vR^iddhAH ta.m lAjaiH paura yoShitaH pR^iShataiH
    mandara uddhUtaiH kShIra Urmaya iva acyutam


    sa yayau prathama.m prAcI.m tulyaH prAcIna barhiShA ahitAn
    anila uddhUtaiH tarjayan iva ketubhiH


    rajobhiH syandana uddhUtaiH gajaiH ca ghana sa.nnibhaiH bhuvaH
    talam iva vyoma kurvan vyoma iva bhUtalam


    pratApaH agre tataH shabdaH parAgaH tat anantaram yayau
    pashcAt ratha Adi iti catuH skandhA iva sA camUH


    maru pR^iShThAni ut ambhA.msi nAvyAH supratarAH nadIH
    vipinAni prakAshAni shaktimattvAt cakAra saH


    saH senA.n mahatI.m karShan pUrva sAgara gAminIm babhau
    hara jaTA bhraShTA.n ga~NgAm iva bhagIrathaH


    tyAjitaiH phalam utkhAtaiH bhagnaiH ca bahudhA nR^ipaiH
    tasya AsIt ulbaNaH mArgaH pAdapaiH iva dantinaH


    paurastyAn evam AkrAman tAn tAn janapadA~n jayI prApa
    tAlI vana shyAmam upakaNTham mahA udadheH


    anamrANA.m samuddhartuH tasmAt sindhu rayAt iva AtmA
    sa.nrakShitaH suhmaiH vR^ittim Ashritya vaitasIm


    va~NgAn utkhAya tarasA netA nau sAdhana udyatAn nicakhAna
    jaya stambhAn ga~NgA srotaH antareShu saH


    A pAda padma praNatAH kalamA iva te raghum phalaiH
    sa.nvardhayAmAsuH utkhAta prati ropitAH


    saH tIrtvA kapishA.m sainyaiH baddha dvirada setubhiH
    utkala adarshita pathaH kali~Nga abhimukhaH yayau


    sa pratApa.m mahendrasya mUrdhni tIkShNa.m nyaveshayat
    a~Nkusha.m dviradasya iva yantA gambhIra vedinaH


    prati jagrAha kAli~NgaH tam astraiH gaja sAdhanaH pakSha
    cCheda udyata.m shakra.m shilA varShI iva parvataH


    dviShA.m viShahya kAkutsthaH tatra nArAca durdinam
    sanma~Ngala snAta iva pratipede jayashriyam


    tAmbUlInA.m dalaiH tatra racita apAna bhUmayaH nArikela
    Asava.m yodhAH shAtrava.m ca papuH yashaH


    gR^ihIta pratimuktasya saH dharma vijayI nR^ipaH shriya.m
    mahendra nAthasya jahAra na tu medinIm


    tataH velA taTena eva phalavat pUgamAlinA agastya AcaritAm
    AshAm an+AshAsya jayaH [san] yayau


    saH sainya paribhogeNa gaja dAna sugandhinA kAverI.m
    saritA.m patyuH sha~NkanIyAm iva akarot


    balaiH adhyuShitA tasya vijigIShoH gata adhvanaH mArIca
    udbhrAnta hArItAH malaya adreH upatyakAH


    sasa~njuH ashva kShuNNAnam elAnAm ut patiShNavaH
    tulya gandhiShu mattebha kaTeShu phala reNavaH


    bhogi veShTana mArgeShu candanAnA.m samarpitam
    na asrasat kariNA.m graiva.m tripadI cChedinAm api


    dishi mandAyate tejaH dakShiNasyA.m raveH api tasyAm
    eva raghoH pANDyAH pratApa.m na viShehire


    tAmraparNI sametasya muktA sAra.n mahA udadheH
    te nipatya daduH tasmai yashaH svam iva sa.ncitam


    saH nirvishya yathA kAma.m taTeShu AlInaH candanau stanau iva 
    dishaH tasyAH shailau malaya dardurau a+sahya vikramaH sahya.m 
    dUrAt muktam udanvatA nitambam iva medinyAH srasta a.nshukam 
    ala~Nghayat


    tasya anIkaiH visarpadbhiH aparAnta jaya udyataiH
    rAma astra utsArita api AsIt sahya lagna iva arNavaH


    bhaya utsR^iShTa vibhUShANA.m tena kerala yoShitAm
    alakeShu camU reNuH cUrNa pratinidhI kR^itaH


    muralA mAruta uddhUtam agamat kaitaka.m rajaH
    tat yodha vAra bANAnAm ayatna paTa vAsatAm


    abhyabhUyata vAhAnA.m caratA.m gAtra shi~njitaiH
    varmabhiH pavana uddhUta rAja tAlI vana dhvaniH


    kharjUrI skandha naddhAnA.m mada udgAra sugandhiShu
    kaTeShu kariNA.m petuH pu.nnAgebhyaH shilI mukhAH


    avakAsha.m kila udanvAn rAmAya abhyarthitaH
    dadau aparAnta mahI pAla vyAjena rAghave karam


    mattebha radana utkIrNa vyakta vikrama lakShaNam
    trikUTam eva tatra ucchaiH jaya stambha.m cakAra saH


    pArasIkA.n tataH jetu.m pratasthe sthala vartmanA
    indriyAkhyAn iva ripU.n tattva j~nAnena sa.myamI


    yavanI mukha padmAnA.m sehe madhu mada.m
    na saH bAla Atapam iva abja AnAm a kAla jalada udayaH


    sa~NgrAmaH tumulaH tasya pAshcAtyaiH ashva
    sAdhanaiH shAr~Nga kUjita vij~neya prati yodhe rajasi abhUt


    bhalla apavarjitaiH teShA.m shirobhiH shmashrulaiH
    mahIm tastAra saraghA vyAptaiH saH kShaudra paTalaiH iva


    apanIta shirastrANAH sheShAH ta.m sharaNa.m
    yayuH praNipAta pratIkAraH sa.mrambhaH hi mahAtmanAm


    vinayante sma tat yodhA madhubhiH vijaya shramam AstIrNa ajina 
    ratnAsu drAkSha Avalaya bhUmiShu


    tataH pratasthe kauberI.m bhAsvAn iva raghuH disham
    sharaiH usraiH iva udIcyAn uddhariShyan rasAn iva


    vinIta adhva shramAH tasya sindhu tIra viceShTanaiH
    dudhuvuH vAjinaH skandhAn lagna ku~Nkuma kesarAn


    tatra hUNa avarodhAnA.m bhartR^iShu vyakta vikramam
    kapola pATala Adeshi babhUva raghu ceShTitam


    kAmbojAH samare soDhu.m tasya vIryam anIshvarAH
    gaja alAna parikliShTaiH akShoTaiH sArdham AnatAH


    teShA.m sat ashva bhUyiShThAH tu~NgA draviNa
    rAshayaH upadAH vivishuH shashvat na utsekAH kosala Ishvaram


    tataH gaurI guru.m shailam Aruroha ashva sAdhanaH
    vardhayan iva tat kUTAn uddhUtaiH dhAtu reNubhiH


    shasha.msa tulya sattvAnA.m sainya ghoShe api asa.nbhramam
    guhA shayAnA.m si.mhAnA.m parivR^itya avalokitam


    bhUrjeShu marmarIbhUtAH kIcaka dhvani hetavaH
    ga~NgA shIkariNaH mArge marutaH ta.n siShevire


    vishashramuH namerUNA.m ChAyAsu adhyAsya sainikAH
    dR^iShadaH vAsita utsa~NgA niShaNNa mR^iga nAbhibhiH


    saralA sakta mAta~Nga graiveya sphurita tviShaH Asan
    oShadhayaH netuH naktam a+sneha dIpikAH


    tasya utsR^iShTa nivAseShu kaNTha rajju kShata tvacaH
    gaja varShma kirAtebhyaH shasha.nsuH devadAravaH


    tatra janya.m raghoH ghora.m parvatIyaiH gaNaiH abhUt
    nArAca kShepaNIya ashma niShpeSha utpatita analam


    sharaiH utsavasa.nketAn saH kR^itvA virata utsavAn
    jaya udAharaNa.m bAhvoH gApayAmAsa ki.nnarAn


    paraspareNa vij~nAtaH teShu upAyana pANiShu rAj~nA himavataH sAraH 
    rAj~naH sAraH hima adriNA


    tatra akShobhya.m yasho rAshi.m niveshya avaruroha
    saH paulastya tulitasya adreH AdadhAna iva hriyam


    cakampe tIrNa lauhitye tasmin prAgjyotiSha IshvaraH
    tat gaja alAnatA.m prAptaiH saha kAla aguru drumaiH


    na prasehe sa ruddha arkam a+dhArA varSha durdinam
    ratha vartma rajaH api asya kuta eva patAkinIm


    tam IshaH kAmarUpANAm atyAkhaNDala vikramam
    bheje bhinna kaTaiH nAgaiH anyAn uparurodha yaiH


    kAmarUpa IshvaraH tasya hema pITha adhi devatAm
    ratna puShpa upahAreNa ChAyam Anarca pAdayoH


    iti jitvA dishaH jiShNuH nyavartata ratha uddhatam
    rajaH vishrAmayan rAj~nA.m Chatra shUnyeShu mauliShu


    saH vishvajitam Ajahre yaj~na.m sarva sva dakShiNam dAna.m hi visargAya satA.m vAri mucAm iva


    satra ante saciva sakhaH puras kriyAbhiH gurvIbhiH shamita 
    parAjaya vyalIkAn
    kAkutsthaH cira viraha utsuka avarodhAn rAjanyAn sva pura 
    nivR^ittaye anumene


    te rekhA dhvaja kulisha Atapatra chihna.m samrAjaH caraNa yuga.m 
    prasAda labhyam
    prasthAna praNatibhiH a~NgulIShu cakruH mauli srak cyuta makaranda 
    reNu gauram
